Developer: Rishika Ratnam

##### **The Core Challenge:**

In today’s fast-paced world, efficient transportation is pivotal. Traditional manual booking systems often struggle with the intricate requirements of both administrators managing a fleet and passengers trying to navigate travel options. There is a critical need for an adaptive system that prioritizes user satisfaction, security, and operational transparency.





##### **Project Vision:**

This project aims to solve these challenges by creating a digital ecosystem that bridges the gap between railway authorities and travelers. 



##### **The Objective Is To Provide:**

For Administrators: A robust backbone to manage train schedules, adjust seat availability in real-time, and handle fare adjustments dynamically.

For Passengers: A convenient, intuitive interface that removes the stress from travel planning by allowing for seamless train discovery and secure ticket management.
